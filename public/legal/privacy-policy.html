Privacy Policy

FarmVault Smart Farm Management Platform

FarmVault is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store and secure it, and what rights you have regarding your data. This policy applies to all users of the FarmVault Platform, including Company Admins, employees, and Ambassadors. By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described herein.

1.0Data Controller Information

FarmVault operates as the data controller for all personal data collected through the Platform. Our contact details for data-related matters are:

FarmVault — Data Protection Officer
Email: privacy@farmvault.africa
Website: www.farmvault.africa

FarmVault processes personal data in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (DPA 2019) and any applicable data protection regulations in jurisdictions where the Platform is operated.

2.0Data We Collect

2.1 Account & Identity Data

  • Full name, email address, and phone number;
  • Business name, farm details, and location information;
  • Profile photograph (if provided);
  • National identification details where required for Ambassador verification.

2.2 Farm & Operational Data

  • Field boundaries, acreage, and crop types;
  • Planting, harvesting, and production records;
  • Input records (fertilisers, pesticides, seeds, labour);
  • Inventory and stock management data;
  • Financial records and budgets entered into the Platform;
  • Employee and staff management records.

2.3 Payment Data

  • M-Pesa phone number and transaction reference numbers;
  • Subscription payment history and amounts.

FarmVault does not store full financial account credentials. Payment processing is handled through M-Pesa and related payment infrastructure operators.

2.4 Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, and device identifiers;
  • Pages visited, features used, and session duration;
  • Error logs and diagnostic data;
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 2.6).

2.5 Communications Data

  • Support tickets, chat messages, and email communications with FarmVault;
  • Feedback, survey responses, and in-app notifications.

2.6 Cookies

The Platform uses essential session cookies to maintain your login state, preference cookies to remember your settings, and analytics cookies to understand Platform usage. You may manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality.

4.0Data Storage & Security

4.1 Storage Infrastructure

All Platform data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted infrastructure. FarmVault selects hosting providers that maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures compliant with international standards.

4.2 Encryption

Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at the storage layer. Payment-related data is handled exclusively through PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment processors.

4.3 Access Controls

Access to your data within the Platform is governed by role-based access control (RBAC). Only authorised FarmVault personnel with a legitimate operational need are permitted to access user data. All such access is logged and audited.

4.4 Security Incident Response

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, FarmVault will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware, and will notify affected users without undue delay, in accordance with applicable law.

4.5 International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside Kenya by our hosting and service providers. Where such transfers occur, FarmVault ensures appropriate safeguards are in place, including contractual commitments equivalent to applicable data protection standards.

Upon expiry of retention periods, data is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymised. You may request early deletion subject to our legal retention obligations (see Section 6.0).

6.0Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and other applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Right of Access

You may request a copy of the personal data FarmVault holds about you. We will respond within 21 days of a verified request.

6.2 Right to Rectification

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. Most profile information can be updated directly in your account settings.

6.3 Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of your personal data where there is no overriding legal basis for retention. Note that certain financial records must be retained for statutory periods regardless of erasure requests.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You may request your farm data in a machine-readable format (CSV or JSON) for transfer to another service. Export requests will be fulfilled within 10 business days.

6.5 Right to Object

You may object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes. We will cease such processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

6.6 How to Exercise Your Rights

Submit all data rights requests to privacy@farmvault.africa with the subject line "Data Rights Request". You may be required to verify your identity before we process your request.

7.0Third-Party Services & Subprocessors

FarmVault uses trusted third-party service providers to operate the Platform. These providers act as data processors and are contractually bound to process your data only on FarmVault's instructions. Current key subprocessors include:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication infrastructure;
  • Clerk — user identity and authentication management;
  • Safaricom M-Pesa — payment processing;
  • Email delivery provider — transactional and notification emails.

An up-to-date list of subprocessors is available on request at privacy@farmvault.africa.

8.0Changes to This Policy

FarmVault may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification at least fourteen (14) days before they take effect. Continued use of the Platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
