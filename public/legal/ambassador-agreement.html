Ambassador Agreement

FarmVault Smart Farm Management Platform — Referral Programme

This Ambassador Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between FarmVault ("Company", "we", "us") and the individual who has enrolled in the FarmVault Ambassador Programme ("Ambassador", "you"). By completing the Ambassador onboarding process on the Platform, you agree to be bound by this Agreement in its entirety. This Agreement governs the terms under which you may refer new subscribers to FarmVault and earn commission on qualifying referrals.

1.0Definitions

"Ambassador"
An individual enrolled in the FarmVault Ambassador Programme who actively promotes the Platform and refers prospective subscribers.
"Referral"
A new subscriber who signs up to a paid FarmVault subscription through the Ambassador's unique referral link or code.
"Qualifying Subscription"
A paid subscription plan purchased by a Referral that meets the minimum criteria specified in Section 3.0.
"Commission"
The monetary amount payable to an Ambassador upon confirmed payment of a Qualifying Subscription by a Referral.
"Ambassador Dashboard"
The dedicated in-Platform section where Ambassadors track referrals, earnings, and payment history.

2.0Eligibility & Onboarding

2.1 Eligibility Requirements

To become a FarmVault Ambassador, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age;
  • Be a Kenyan resident or otherwise legally eligible to receive M-Pesa payments;
  • Possess a valid national identification document (National ID or Passport);
  • Register a valid M-Pesa-enabled mobile number for commission disbursements;
  • Complete the Ambassador onboarding wizard on the Platform.

2.2 Identity Verification

FarmVault reserves the right to request identity verification documents before processing any commission payment. Providing false, expired, or fraudulent identification documents will result in immediate termination of Ambassador status and forfeiture of all unpaid commissions.

2.3 Ambassador Profile

Upon completing onboarding, you will be issued a unique referral link and assigned an Ambassador profile on the Platform. You are responsible for keeping your profile information accurate and up to date, including your M-Pesa number.

3.0Commission Structure

3.1 Qualifying Subscriptions

A commission is earned only when a Referral purchases a paid subscription plan through the Ambassador's unique referral link and payment is successfully confirmed in the FarmVault system. Free tier activations and trial periods do not qualify for commission.

3.2 Commission Rates

Commission rates are set by FarmVault and are displayed in the Ambassador Dashboard at the time of each qualifying event. Current rates are as follows:

Subscription Plan Commission Type Rate
Monthly Subscription One-time per referral (first month) As published on Ambassador Dashboard
Annual Subscription One-time per referral As published on Ambassador Dashboard
Renewal (subsequent months) Recurring (while referral subscription is active) As published on Ambassador Dashboard

FarmVault reserves the right to modify commission rates with fourteen (14) days' notice to Ambassadors. Rate changes do not apply retroactively to commissions already earned.

3.3 Attribution & Cookies

A Referral is attributed to you when a prospective user clicks your unique referral link and completes a paid subscription. Attribution is tracked for thirty (30) days from the initial click. If a user visits the Platform through multiple referral links, attribution is assigned to the most recent link clicked before subscription.

3.4 No Self-Referrals

You may not use your own referral link to create new accounts for your own business or for businesses you own, control, or are employed by. Self-referrals are strictly prohibited and will result in forfeiture of any commissions earned and potential termination.

4.0Payment Conditions

4.1 Payment Method

All commission payments are made via M-Pesa to the phone number registered in your Ambassador profile. You are responsible for ensuring your payment details are accurate. FarmVault is not liable for payments sent to incorrect numbers resulting from inaccurate profile information.

4.2 Payment Threshold

Commissions are disbursed when your unpaid balance reaches the minimum threshold of KES 500. Balances below this threshold are carried forward to the next payment cycle.

4.3 Payment Schedule

Qualifying commissions are calculated and disbursed on the last business day of each calendar month, subject to the minimum threshold being met. The payment schedule may be adjusted by FarmVault with seven (7) days' advance notice.

4.4 Commission Hold Period

Newly earned commissions are subject to a fourteen (14) day hold period from the date the Referral's payment is confirmed. This allows time to identify and reverse fraudulent or cancelled subscriptions before commission disbursement.

4.5 Commission Reversal

FarmVault may reverse or withhold commission if a Referral's subscription is cancelled, charged back, or found to be fraudulent within sixty (60) days of the qualifying payment. Reversed commissions will be deducted from your next payment or recovered directly.

4.6 Tax Obligations

You are solely responsible for declaring and paying any taxes applicable to your commission income in your jurisdiction, including withholding tax, income tax, or VAT as applicable under Kenyan law. FarmVault does not deduct or remit taxes on behalf of Ambassadors unless required by law.

5.0Non-Employment Clause

Independent Contractor Status: The Ambassador relationship is that of an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, agency, partnership, or joint venture relationship between you and FarmVault.

As an Ambassador, you:

  • Are not an employee, agent, or representative of FarmVault;
  • Have no authority to enter into contracts or make representations on behalf of FarmVault;
  • Are not entitled to employment benefits, including paid leave, health insurance, or pension;
  • Are solely responsible for your own expenses, taxes, and statutory obligations;
  • May carry out activities for other companies, provided they do not compete directly with FarmVault or breach any confidentiality obligations herein.

6.0Fraud Prevention

6.1 Prohibited Conduct

The following actions constitute fraud and are strictly prohibited:

  • Creating fictitious or multiple accounts to generate artificial referrals;
  • Paying subscription fees on behalf of Referrals using your own funds with the intent to earn commission;
  • Using automated bots, scripts, or click-farms to generate referral clicks;
  • Misrepresenting FarmVault's products, pricing, or capabilities to prospective Referrals;
  • Offering cash-back, side payments, or rebates to prospective Referrals in exchange for sign-ups;
  • Manipulating the attribution system through technical means;
  • Colluding with other Ambassadors or third parties to inflate referral counts.

6.2 Investigation & Consequences

FarmVault employs fraud detection systems and reserves the right to investigate any anomalous referral patterns. If fraud is confirmed:

  • All unpaid commissions will be forfeited;
  • Previously paid commissions deemed fraudulent may be recovered;
  • Ambassador status will be permanently revoked;
  • Accounts involved will be suspended;
  • Legal action may be pursued where losses are material.

6.3 Reporting

You are encouraged to report suspected fraudulent activity by other Ambassadors to integrity@farmvault.africa. Reports are treated confidentially.

7.0Code of Conduct & Marketing Standards

As a FarmVault Ambassador, you represent the FarmVault brand. You agree to:

  • Promote FarmVault honestly and accurately, based on publicly available information;
  • Not make promises or guarantees about the Platform's capabilities that exceed what is documented;
  • Not use spam, unsolicited messaging, or deceptive marketing practices;
  • Clearly disclose your role as an Ambassador when promoting the Platform;
  • Comply with all applicable advertising standards and consumer protection laws;
  • Not disparage competitors using false or misleading statements.

FarmVault reserves the right to request removal of any Ambassador marketing materials that violate these standards.

8.0Termination Rights

8.1 Termination by Ambassador

You may resign from the Ambassador Programme at any time by notifying FarmVault at ambassadors@farmvault.africa. Any commissions earned and cleared prior to resignation will be paid on the next scheduled payment date. Commissions in the hold period at the time of resignation will be paid upon clearing.

8.2 Termination by FarmVault

FarmVault may terminate your Ambassador status at any time, with or without cause. If termination is without cause, any earned and cleared commissions will be paid. If termination is for cause (fraud, breach of agreement, or misconduct), unpaid commissions may be forfeited.

8.3 Post-Termination

Upon termination, your referral link will be deactivated. Subscriptions already attributed to you prior to termination will continue to generate recurring commissions for a maximum of six (6) months, unless terminated for cause.

9.0Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Kenya. Disputes shall be resolved as per the dispute resolution mechanism outlined in the FarmVault Terms of Service.
