Acceptable Use Policy

FarmVault Smart Farm Management Platform

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the rules and standards governing how users may and may not use the FarmVault Platform. It exists to protect the Platform's integrity, safeguard all users, and ensure a productive environment for the farming community. By accessing the Platform, you agree to comply with this AUP at all times. Violations may result in immediate access restriction, account suspension, or legal action.

1.0Permitted Use

The FarmVault Platform is provided exclusively for lawful agricultural management activities. Permitted uses include:

  • Managing and recording farm operations, crop cycles, field activities, and harvests;
  • Tracking farm inputs, inventory, and expenditure;
  • Managing employee and team member access for your registered entity;
  • Generating reports and analytics for your own business decision-making;
  • Participating in the Ambassador Programme in accordance with the Ambassador Agreement;
  • Communicating with FarmVault support through designated channels.

All use must be in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, and the FarmVault Terms of Service.

2.0Platform Misuse Rules

2.1 Account Misuse

You must not:

  • Share your account credentials with individuals outside your registered business entity;
  • Create multiple accounts to circumvent subscription limitations or enforcement actions;
  • Register a business entity that does not exist, is fictitious, or is registered by someone other than yourself without their authority;
  • Impersonate another person, business, or FarmVault employee in any Platform interaction.

2.2 Data Misuse

You must not:

  • Upload, store, or transmit data that infringes third-party intellectual property rights;
  • Use the Platform to process personal data of third parties without appropriate legal authority;
  • Scrape, harvest, or systematically extract data from the Platform using automated means;
  • Access or attempt to access another user's account or data without authorisation;
  • Use data obtained from the Platform for purposes other than your own internal farm management.

2.3 System Misuse

You must not:

  • Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of any FarmVault system or network;
  • Attempt to bypass, circumvent, or undermine any security or access control mechanism;
  • Attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble any part of the Platform software;
  • Introduce, upload, or transmit any virus, malware, worm, trojan, or other malicious code;
  • Interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of the Platform or its underlying infrastructure.

3.0Prohibited Behaviour

Zero Tolerance: The following categories of behaviour result in immediate account suspension pending investigation. Some may also result in criminal or civil legal proceedings.

3.1 Illegal Activity

You must not use the Platform in connection with any activity that is illegal under Kenyan law or applicable international law, including but not limited to money laundering, tax evasion, fraud, or any activity that violates agricultural regulations.

3.2 Harassment & Abuse

You must not use the Platform to harass, threaten, intimidate, or abuse FarmVault staff, other users, or third parties. This includes through support channels, reporting features, or any in-Platform communication facility.

3.3 Fraudulent Transactions

You must not submit fraudulent, forged, or fictitious payment references. You must not manipulate the billing system to obtain subscription access without valid payment. All detected fraud is investigated and reported to relevant authorities.

3.4 Unauthorised Commercial Use

You must not use the Platform to provide services commercially to third parties (e.g., operating a farm management bureau service for other farms) without entering into a commercial reseller or partner agreement with FarmVault.

3.5 Intellectual Property Violations

You must not copy, reproduce, distribute, sublicence, sell, or create derivative works from any part of the FarmVault Platform, including its design, code, documentation, or trademarks, without explicit written authorisation from FarmVault.

3.6 Harmful Content

You must not upload content that is defamatory, obscene, offensive, discriminatory, or in violation of any applicable content standards. FarmVault reserves the right to remove any content that violates this provision without notice.

3.7 API & Integration Abuse

Where API access is provided, you must not exceed authorised rate limits, use the API for purposes outside those specified in the API documentation, or share API credentials with unauthorised parties.

4.0Abuse Prevention

4.1 Monitoring

FarmVault employs automated and manual monitoring systems to detect potential abuse, unusual patterns, and policy violations. By using the Platform, you acknowledge and consent to this monitoring for the purpose of security, fraud prevention, and policy enforcement.

4.2 Reporting Abuse

If you discover a potential security vulnerability, witness suspected abuse, or believe another user is violating this AUP, you are encouraged to report it to:

Email: security@farmvault.africa
Subject: AUP Violation Report / Security Vulnerability Report
Response SLA: Within 48 business hours

All reports are treated confidentially. FarmVault operates a responsible disclosure programme for security vulnerabilities — legitimate security researchers will not face enforcement action for good-faith, non-destructive testing with prior notification.

4.3 Rate Limits

FarmVault enforces rate limits on API calls, data submissions, and login attempts to protect the Platform from abuse. Deliberately circumventing rate limits through technical means constitutes a violation of this AUP.

4.4 Denial of Service

Any action intended to degrade, disrupt, or deny service to other users or to the Platform infrastructure — including distributed denial of service (DDoS) attacks, resource exhaustion attacks, or deliberate bulk data operations — is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate termination and legal action.

5.0Enforcement Actions

5.1 Graduated Response

FarmVault applies a graduated enforcement approach proportionate to the severity and frequency of violations:

Severity Action Notice
Minor / First Offence Written warning via email With warning
Moderate / Repeated Temporary feature restriction (24–72 hours) With restriction notice
Serious Account suspension pending investigation At suspension or within 24 hours
Severe (fraud, illegal activity) Immediate permanent termination Notification following investigation
Criminal Report to law enforcement authorities As required by law

5.2 Right to Appeal

Users who believe an enforcement action was applied in error may appeal in writing to compliance@farmvault.africa within fourteen (14) days of the enforcement notification. Appeals are reviewed by a senior member of the FarmVault compliance team within seven (7) business days.

5.3 No Refunds for Enforcement Terminations

Accounts terminated for AUP violations are not entitled to refunds of prepaid subscription fees. FarmVault may also pursue recovery of costs associated with investigating or mitigating serious violations.

5.4 Amendments to This Policy

FarmVault may update this AUP at any time with fourteen (14) days' notice. Continued use of the Platform after the effective date of any update constitutes acceptance.
