Subscription & Billing Policy

FarmVault Smart Farm Management Platform

This Subscription & Billing Policy ("Policy") sets out the rules governing subscription payments, renewal, expiry, and pricing for the FarmVault Platform. It supplements the Terms of Service and applies to all paying subscribers. By purchasing a subscription, you agree to be bound by this Policy. Please read it carefully before initiating any payment.

1.0Subscription Plans Overview

1.1 Available Plans

FarmVault offers subscription plans published on the Platform's pricing page. Plans vary by features, user limits, field capacity, and billing period. FarmVault reserves the right to introduce, modify, or retire plans with reasonable notice to existing subscribers.

1.2 Billing Periods

Subscriptions are available on monthly and annual billing cycles. Annual plans are prepaid for twelve (12) months in a single payment. Monthly plans are prepaid for one (1) calendar month in advance.

1.3 Currency

All subscription prices are quoted and payable in Kenyan Shillings (KES) unless otherwise stated. Prices displayed to users in other currencies are for indicative purposes only; the billing currency remains KES.

2.0Manual Payment Rules

Manual Payment: A payment made directly via M-Pesa to the FarmVault business number, followed by submission of the payment reference within the Platform.

2.1 How to Pay Manually

  1. Send the exact subscription amount via M-Pesa to the FarmVault Paybill or Till Number displayed in your account's billing section.
  2. Use your Account ID or registered email as the account reference.
  3. Navigate to Billing → Manual Payment in your FarmVault account.
  4. Enter the M-Pesa Transaction Code (e.g., QABCXYZ12) and payment amount.
  5. Submit the payment confirmation form.

2.2 Verification & Activation

Manual payments are verified by the FarmVault billing team within one (1) to four (4) business hours during business days (Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00 EAT). Payments submitted outside business hours will be processed on the next business day. Subscription access is activated upon successful verification.

2.3 Exact Amount Required

You must remit the exact published subscription amount. Underpayments will not activate the subscription and will be held pending top-up or refund request. Overpayments will be credited to your account balance for future billing cycles. FarmVault is not responsible for lost funds resulting from payments to incorrect M-Pesa numbers.

2.4 Valid Transaction Codes

Each transaction code may only be used once. Attempting to reuse a transaction code or submitting a code from a payment made to a different number or entity is treated as fraud and will result in account suspension and a formal investigation.

2.5 Manual Payment Deadline

Manual payment references must be submitted within forty-eight (48) hours of making the M-Pesa transfer. After 48 hours, unsubmitted payments require support intervention to resolve. Contact billing@farmvault.africa with your transaction code and date of payment.

3.0STK Push Payment Rules

STK Push: An automated payment prompt sent directly to your M-Pesa registered mobile phone, initiated from within the FarmVault Platform.

3.1 How STK Push Works

  1. Select your subscription plan in the FarmVault billing section.
  2. Enter your M-Pesa registered phone number.
  3. Click "Pay via M-Pesa" — a payment prompt will be sent to your phone within seconds.
  4. Enter your M-Pesa PIN on your phone to authorise the payment.
  5. Your subscription will be activated automatically upon payment confirmation.

3.2 Automatic Activation

STK Push payments are confirmed in real time via the Safaricom M-Pesa API. Upon successful payment, your subscription is activated instantly without manual verification. You will receive a confirmation SMS from M-Pesa and an in-app notification from FarmVault.

3.3 Failed STK Push Requests

If the STK Push prompt does not arrive within 60 seconds, do not re-initiate — wait at least two (2) minutes before trying again to avoid duplicate charges. Common reasons for failure include poor network connectivity, M-Pesa service outages, or insufficient M-Pesa balance.

3.4 Duplicate Payment Protection

FarmVault implements idempotency controls to prevent double-charging. If a duplicate charge is detected, the additional amount will be automatically credited to your account balance or refunded within five (5) business days. Report suspected duplicates to billing@farmvault.africa immediately.

3.5 Phone Number Requirements

The phone number used for STK Push must be a registered and active Safaricom M-Pesa number. FarmVault cannot initiate STK Push to non-Safaricom numbers or numbers that have not activated M-Pesa services.

4.0Renewal Policy

4.1 Renewal Responsibility

FarmVault does not automatically charge subscriptions. All renewals are manual or require explicit user-initiated STK Push or manual payment. It is your responsibility to renew your subscription before the expiry date to maintain uninterrupted access.

4.2 Renewal Reminders

FarmVault will send renewal reminders via email and in-app notification at fourteen (14) days, seven (7) days, three (3) days, and on the day of expiry. Failure to receive reminder notifications does not exempt you from the obligation to renew. Ensure your contact details are kept current in your account settings.

4.3 Grace Period

Upon subscription expiry, a grace period of seven (7) calendar days is granted during which you retain read-only access to your data. During the grace period, you cannot add new records, use premium features, or invite new users. Full access is restored immediately upon successful renewal payment.

4.4 Renewal Date

Renewing during or after the grace period does not extend the subscription retroactively. Your new billing period starts from the date of payment confirmation. If you renewed on the 15th for a monthly plan, your next expiry date is the 15th of the following month.

4.5 Plan Upgrades & Downgrades

You may upgrade your plan at any time by paying the difference between your current plan and the higher plan (prorated for the remaining billing period). Downgrades take effect at the next renewal cycle. Mid-cycle downgrades are not supported.

5.0Expiry Logic

5.1 Subscription Expiry

A subscription expires at 23:59:59 EAT on the last day of the billing period. After expiry, the Platform automatically restricts premium features. The following table summarises access states:

State Days After Expiry Access Level
Active N/A (within billing period) Full access to all subscribed features
Grace Period Days 1–7 after expiry Read-only; no new records; no new users
Expired Day 8+ after expiry Login only; data visible but locked
Scheduled Deletion Day 91+ (without renewal) Account scheduled for data deletion

5.2 Data During Expiry

Your farm data is preserved for ninety (90) days after expiry to allow for renewal or data export. After ninety (90) days without renewal, FarmVault will issue a final deletion notice. Data will be permanently deleted thirty (30) days after the deletion notice unless you renew or request export.

5.3 Reactivation After Expiry

You may reactivate your subscription at any time within the ninety (90) day data retention window by making a new payment. All historical data will be restored upon reactivation.

6.0Pricing Change Clause

6.1 Price Stability

FarmVault endeavours to maintain subscription pricing stability. Any price increases will be communicated to existing subscribers no less than thirty (30) days before the change takes effect.

6.2 Price Change Notification

Price change notifications will be delivered via email to your registered address and via in-app notification. The notification will specify the new pricing, the effective date, and how the change affects your current plan.

6.3 Existing Subscriptions

Price changes apply to renewal payments only. Your current prepaid billing period will not be affected by a price change. The new price will apply from your next renewal date.

6.4 Right to Cancel

If you disagree with a pricing change, you may cancel your subscription before the effective date and your data will be retained for ninety (90) days from expiry as per Section 5.2. Cancellation requests must be submitted to billing@farmvault.africa.

7.0Refund Policy

Subscription fees are generally non-refundable. A refund may be considered in the following limited circumstances:

  • A confirmed technical failure on FarmVault's part that prevented service access for more than 24 continuous hours;
  • A verified duplicate payment;
  • A payment made in error to a valid FarmVault payment channel, reported within 24 hours.

Refund requests must be submitted to billing@farmvault.africa within seven (7) days of the payment. FarmVault will review and respond within seven (7) business days. Approved refunds are processed via the original M-Pesa number within five (5) business days.
