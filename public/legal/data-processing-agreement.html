Data Processing Agreement

FarmVault Smart Farm Management Platform

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the contractual relationship between FarmVault ("Processor", "we", "us") and the registered business entity using the Platform ("Controller", "you"). It governs how FarmVault processes personal data on behalf of the Controller in connection with the delivery of Platform services. This DPA is entered into automatically upon acceptance of the Terms of Service and supplements both the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

1.0Definitions & Interpretation

"Personal Data"
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.
"Processing"
Any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, storage, retrieval, alteration, disclosure, erasure, and any other use or manipulation.
"Controller"
The registered business entity that determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data — i.e., the FarmVault customer using the Platform to manage their farm and employees.
"Processor"
FarmVault, which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller solely to deliver the Platform services.
"Data Subject"
An identified or identifiable natural person whose Personal Data is processed — including farm employees, managers, or other individuals whose details are entered into the Platform.
"Subprocessor"
A third-party service provider engaged by FarmVault to assist in processing Personal Data in connection with Platform services.
"DPA"
This Data Processing Agreement.
"KDPA"
The Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and associated regulations.

2.0Data Ownership

2.1 Controller's Data

The Controller retains full ownership of all Personal Data entered into the Platform relating to their employees, operations, and business. FarmVault does not claim any ownership right over Personal Data provided by or generated on behalf of the Controller.

2.2 Processor's Role

FarmVault processes Controller Personal Data solely in its capacity as a Processor, acting on the documented instructions of the Controller as set out in this DPA, the Terms of Service, and as instructed through Platform features. FarmVault shall not process Controller Personal Data for any purpose beyond what is necessary to deliver the Platform services, unless required by law.

2.3 Platform-Generated Data

Aggregated, anonymised, or de-identified analytical data derived from Platform usage (where it cannot be linked back to any individual or the Controller's specific business) may be used by FarmVault for product improvement and research purposes. Such use does not affect the Controller's ownership of the underlying Personal Data.

2.4 Data Inventory

The categories of Personal Data processed by FarmVault on behalf of the Controller include:

Category Data Types Data Subjects
Identity & Contact Name, phone number, email address Farm employees, managers
Employment Records Role, employment dates, payroll information Farm employees
Operational Records Task assignments, work records, attendance Farm employees, managers
Access Data Login timestamps, IP addresses, device information Platform users (employees, admins)
Financial Records Wage records, payment confirmations Farm employees

3.0Processing Obligations

3.1 Instructions-Only Processing

FarmVault shall process Controller Personal Data only in accordance with the Controller's documented instructions as reflected in this DPA and the Terms of Service. If FarmVault is required by applicable law to process Controller Personal Data in a manner beyond these instructions, FarmVault will inform the Controller before such processing unless legally prohibited from doing so.

3.2 Confidentiality

FarmVault shall ensure that all personnel authorised to process Controller Personal Data are bound by appropriate confidentiality obligations, whether by contract or statutory duty. Access to Controller Personal Data is restricted to personnel who require it for the performance of their duties.

3.3 Controller Responsibilities

The Controller is responsible for:

  • Ensuring that Personal Data entered into the Platform is collected lawfully and with appropriate consent or legal basis;
  • Providing adequate privacy notices to Data Subjects whose data is entered into the Platform;
  • Obtaining any necessary permissions or consents required to process employee data using the Platform;
  • Ensuring that Personal Data is accurate, relevant, and not excessive for the stated purpose.

3.4 Assistance with Compliance

FarmVault shall provide reasonable assistance to the Controller in fulfilling its obligations under applicable data protection law, including responding to Data Subject rights requests, conducting data protection impact assessments, and notifying relevant authorities of data breaches, to the extent that such assistance relates to data processed by FarmVault on the Controller's behalf.

4.0Security Measures

4.1 Technical Safeguards

FarmVault implements and maintains the following technical security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmissions between users and the Platform are encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher;
  • Encryption at Rest: Stored data is encrypted using AES-256 at the database and storage layer;
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures users access only data appropriate to their role;
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication is available and encouraged for all user accounts;
  • Audit Logging: All access to and modifications of Controller Personal Data are logged with timestamps and user identifiers;
  • Vulnerability Management: FarmVault conducts regular security assessments and patches known vulnerabilities promptly.

4.2 Organisational Safeguards

FarmVault implements the following organisational measures:

  • Security awareness training for all staff with access to personal data;
  • A documented incident response and business continuity plan;
  • Background checks on staff in roles with elevated data access;
  • Regular internal security reviews and access reviews;
  • A designated Data Protection Officer (DPO) responsible for compliance oversight.

4.3 Security Review

FarmVault conducts annual security reviews of its systems and processes. The Controller may request a summary of security measures by submitting a written request to privacy@farmvault.africa. FarmVault will provide a security summary document within ten (10) business days.

5.2 Subprocessor Obligations

All Subprocessors are bound by written data processing agreements that impose obligations at least equivalent to those in this DPA. FarmVault remains fully responsible for the acts and omissions of Subprocessors in relation to Controller Personal Data.

5.3 Subprocessor Changes

FarmVault will provide the Controller with at least thirty (30) days' prior notice before engaging a new Subprocessor or making a material change to an existing Subprocessor's engagement. The Controller may object to such changes in writing within fourteen (14) days of notification. If the Controller's objection cannot be accommodated, either party may terminate the DPA with sixty (60) days' written notice.

5.4 Subprocessor List

An up-to-date list of Subprocessors is available at www.farmvault.africa/legal or upon written request to privacy@farmvault.africa.

6.0Data Subject Rights

Where the Controller receives a Data Subject rights request (access, correction, erasure, portability, or objection) relating to Personal Data processed through the Platform, FarmVault shall:

  • Promptly inform the Controller if FarmVault receives such a request directly from a Data Subject;
  • Provide reasonable technical assistance to enable the Controller to respond to Data Subject requests within the statutory timeframe;
  • Not respond to Data Subject requests directly without the Controller's prior written authorisation, except where required by law.

The Controller is responsible for managing and responding to Data Subject rights requests in relation to data it controls. The Platform provides export, deletion, and correction tools to assist with fulfilment.

7.0Compliance Obligations

7.1 Applicable Law

Both parties shall comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and all applicable subsidiary legislation. Where the Controller operates in jurisdictions with additional data protection requirements, the Controller is responsible for ensuring their use of the Platform is compliant with those additional requirements.

7.2 Data Protection Impact Assessments

Where the Controller is required by law to conduct a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) relating to Processing activities carried out on the Platform, FarmVault will provide reasonable information and assistance upon written request.

7.3 Audit Rights

The Controller may, at its own cost and with thirty (30) days' prior written notice, request an audit or inspection of FarmVault's data processing activities as they relate to Controller Personal Data. FarmVault may satisfy such audit requests by providing a recent third-party security audit report, SOC 2 equivalent, or comparable compliance documentation in lieu of an on-site inspection.

8.0Breach Notification

8.1 FarmVault's Notification Obligation

In the event of a confirmed Personal Data Breach affecting Controller Personal Data, FarmVault shall notify the Controller without undue delay and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware of the breach. The notification shall include, to the extent available:

  • Nature of the breach and categories of data affected;
  • Approximate number of Data Subjects and records affected;
  • Likely consequences of the breach;
  • Measures taken or proposed to address the breach.

8.2 Controller's Notification Obligation

The Controller is responsible for notifying the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) and affected Data Subjects as required by the KDPA, based on the information provided by FarmVault. FarmVault will provide reasonable assistance in preparing these notifications.

9.0Term, Termination & Return of Data

9.1 Term

This DPA remains in force for the duration of the Controller's subscription to the Platform and for as long as FarmVault retains Controller Personal Data pursuant to its retention obligations.

9.2 Return & Deletion

Upon termination of the subscription or upon written request by the Controller, FarmVault shall, within ninety (90) days:

  • Provide the Controller with an export of their data in a standard machine-readable format (CSV or JSON); and
  • Permanently delete all copies of Controller Personal Data from FarmVault's systems, except where retention is required by law.

FarmVault shall provide the Controller with written confirmation of deletion upon completion.

9.3 Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the laws of Kenya. Any disputes arising under this DPA shall be resolved in accordance with the dispute resolution mechanism in the Terms of Service.
