## Short summary
FarmVault is Kenyan agriculture SaaS for farm management: crop and project planning, farm workers, harvest tracking, expenses, inventory, reporting, and multi-farm operations. Pricing is available in Kenyan Shillings (KES). Built for smallholders, cooperatives, and growing agribusinesses.

## Location
- Headquarters focus: Kenya (Nairobi)
- Area served: Kenya first; relevant across East Africa and the continent for comparable farming systems

## Product description
FarmVault is a web-based farm management platform that replaces scattered notebooks and spreadsheets with one place to run the farm. Teams log work and harvest in the field (mobile-friendly), managers see costs and yields by crop or block, and records stay organised for buyers, lenders, or audits.
